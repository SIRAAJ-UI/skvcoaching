About us

Why SKV Coaching Centre?

To empower the new generation through quality education regardless of the economic status, Venkateswar trust was founded in Tiruchengode in the year 2005. The trust, as a first venture, has started SKV Educational Institutions to provide holistic education by imbibing ethical values. With sheer commitment to promote the student community,SKV institution has set a benchmark which no other academic power has hitherto achieved by securing the first three ranks in higher secondary board examinations.

It has set as a trend to send 100s of students to various medical colleges in Tamil nadu every year and we take pride in the fact that our students are studying in all the leading medical and engineering colleges across the state.

Since educational reformers have brought in new All India Entrance Examinations for every field of higher education, our students are forced to travel across the country to identify right coaching centre to train for NEET/AIMS/JIPMER and JEE examinations. On seeing this, we are struck with the mental agony and as a result, SKV has stretched its paramount wings seeking excellence in the vista of education to rise equal to the changing educational scenario with the advent of SKV ACADEMY.

SKV Academy is committed to turning student's skills, creativity, passion and smart work into success with a never before unique model of curriculum design called "study made easy" to shatter the myth of the students that cracking NEET/AIMS/JIPMER/JEE exam is too difficult.

Key Features

  • SKV follows an Integrated Curriculum based on the real requirements of the students, amalgamating State board and Competitive Exams preparation to cover maximum topics in a much optimised time frame so as to allow the students to devote much of their time for studies, thus ensures stress-free preparation.
  • We have recruited Eminent Efficient Subject Experts from leading colleges and coaching centres of various district headquarters of Andhra Pradesh and Kota district of Rajasthan who have ample experience and expertise of 20 years in nurturing the students for AIPMT, NEET and JEE examinations.
  • To improve the study experience, we provide state-of-the-art technologies with the facilities for virtual and e-learning. E-library brings all the available materials under one roof in the form of e-books and provides access to immediate availability of new materials, digital periodicals, academic journals, audio books and global forums. E-lab is based on the concept of ‘learning by doing’ by providing real scientific experiments visually. All our class rooms are equipped with latest learning tools which give access to experts' video classes and live interaction with the experts.
  • Well organised, high structured and exhaustive study materials are meticulously prepared by our experts with theory lessons interspersed with illustrations, integrated mind maps and revision notes which are not only comprehensive but also simplified to make a smooth transition from fundamental to advanced level of learning. Since practice is the main key to success, we also provide effective question banks with last 20 years of AIPMT / JEE / NEET question papers, solved and unsolved problems and sure shot questions.
  • Our R & D team of illustrious faculties includes experts from various domains of science, engineering and medicine who tirelessly labour to prepare updated, improvised study materials and different types, levels of question sets to suit the current trends of competitive exams and upcoming challenges.
  • Since instant removal of each doubt of every student is our top priority, SKV Academy brings in a dedicated Doubt Removal System which is embedded in the day-to-day curriculum so as to remove the doubts of particular day on the same day itself. In order to make the doubt removal system effective, sessions are conducted in bilingual and senior teachers are available in the campus up to 10 PM every day.
  • Technology based Test and Analysis system is followed to achieve expected learning outcomes in the competitive world. Every day, chapter wise and full tests are conducted both in online & offline mode (OMR Coding System) and the result & analysis of which will be send to the parents/guardian on the same day through e-mail, SMS or Whatsapp. System generated analysis comes with the special features like Time based analysis, Difficulty level analysis and Topic wise analysis to identify strong and weak areas of the students for further improvements and to calculate the exact amount of time taken by a student to answer the particular level of question so as to follow effective time management during the time of exam. Comparison with previous year NEET toppers assesses the preparedness of the students and motivates them to perform according to the requirement.
  • To inculcate positivity in the students, SKV experts offer Career counseling, Personal counseling and Motivational counselling by helping them decide the streams they should choose, mentoring them in tough times and boosting the morale and instilling the confidence that NEET / JEE exams can be cracked easily with strategic planning even by a below average student.
  • The Foundation Courses offered at SKV Academy for the students of VI to X std aims to groom them for prestigious Mathematics and Science Olympiads and lays foundation for cracking National Level Entrance Exams like JEE, NEET, AIMS and JIPMER.
